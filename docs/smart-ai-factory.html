Why Pattern Matching Over LLM?

For industrial B2B sales, customers ask predictable questions about products, pricing, and compliance. Pattern matching delivers instant, consistent, accurate responses without the costs, latency, or unpredictability of large language models.

Intent Classification System

The AI recognizes 24 distinct intents, each with optimized regex patterns and entity extraction rules. Patterns support English, Afrikaans, and common South African terminology.

Intent Description Example Queries
PRODUCT_SEARCH Find products by name, category, or code "I need M12 bolts", "Ek soek boute", "Show safety helmets"
PRICE_QUOTE Get pricing for specific products "How much for 500 units?", "Price on safety boots"
COMPLIANCE_CHECK Verify product meets industry standards "Is this SANS 1473 certified?", "Mining compliant?"
SPEC_QUERY Get technical specifications "What size is this bolt?", "Material specs"
DELIVERY_INQUIRY Delivery times and costs "Delivery to Johannesburg?", "Ship to Durban"
BULK_DISCOUNT Volume pricing information "Discount for 1000 units?", "Bulk pricing"
BBBEE_INQUIRY BBB-EE certification questions "What's your BEE level?", "BBB-EE certificate"
STOCK_CHECK Product availability "Is this in stock?", "Availability"
ORDER_STATUS Track existing orders "Where is my order ORD-001234?"
CONTACT_REQUEST Request human contact "Speak to sales", "Contact us"

Entity Extraction

The system automatically extracts structured data from natural language:

  • Quantities: "500 units", "100 pieces", "50"
  • Product Codes: SKU-001, M12-HEX-ZN, BOL-001
  • Order Numbers: ORD-123456, #12345
  • Locations: Johannesburg, Gauteng, Cape Town
  • SANS Codes: SANS 1700, SANS-1473, ISO 9001
  • Industries: Mining, Construction, Electrical

Lead Scoring System

The scoring engine silently tracks visitor behavior and assigns points to calculate lead quality. Scores are persisted to the database and available for the sales dashboard.

Event Type Points Description
quote_submitted +50 Customer submitted a quote request
contact_form_filled +40 Contact form completed
bbbee_cert_request +30 Requested BBB-EE certificate
compliance_check +25 Checked product compliance
chat_price_inquiry +20 Asked about pricing in chat
product_added_to_cart +15 Added product to quote cart
chat_product_inquiry +10 Asked about products in chat
page_view +1 Viewed a page

Lead Tier Classification

HOT (80+ points)

High-intent buyer ready for sales contact. Multiple price inquiries, quote submissions, or BBB-EE requests.

WARM (40-79 points)

Engaged prospect with product interest. Has asked about specs, pricing, or compliance.

COOL (20-39 points)

Early-stage interest. Browsing products, initial chat interactions.

COLD (<20 points)

New or inactive visitor. Minimal engagement so far.

Compliance Verification

Products are verified against South African standards and industry-specific regulations. The system maintains a database of SANS standards, OHSA requirements, and DMR specifications.

Supported Standards

Standard Name Applicable To
SANS 1700 Threaded Fasteners Bolts, Nuts, Screws
SANS 719 Steel Pipes Pipes, Fittings
SANS 10142-1 Electrical Installations Cables, Conduits
SANS 1473 Industrial Safety Helmets Hard Hats, Bump Caps
SANS 1360 Safety Footwear Safety Boots, Shoes
SANS 50 Welding Products Rods, Wire, Flux

Industry Requirements

Mining (DMR/OHSA)

SANS 1473, SANS 1360, SANS 1700 mandatory. All PPE must meet Mine Health & Safety Act requirements.

Construction (NHBRC)

SANS 1700, SANS 719 for structural components. CIDB grading compliance for contractors.

Electrical (NRCS)

SANS 10142-1, NRCS Letter of Authority required. Cables must meet IEC standards.
