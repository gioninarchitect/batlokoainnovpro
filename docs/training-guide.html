Complete guide to managing your business

Welcome to Batlokoa Admin

This training guide covers everything you need to know to effectively manage orders, customers, products, quotes, and invoices in the Batlokoa Innovative Projects admin system.

Navigate to Admin Login

Go to /admin/login or click "Admin" from the main website footer.

2

Enter Credentials

Enter your email address and password, then click "Sign in".

3

Dashboard Access

Upon successful login, you'll be redirected to the Dashboard.

Tip: Use the dark mode toggle (sun/moon icon) in the top right to switch themes.

The Dashboard provides a quick overview of your business metrics:

Stats Cards

CardShows
RevenueTotal sales revenue
OrdersTotal orders + pending/processing counts
CustomersTotal registered customers
ProductsTotal products + low stock alerts

Alerts Panel

Color-coded alerts appear at the top when action is needed:

  • Yellow - POP awaiting verification
  • Blue - Quotes expiring soon
  • Red - Low stock / Overdue invoices

Quick Actions

Use the bottom grid to quickly create new orders, quotes, customers, or products.

Creating a New Order

1

Click "New Order"

From Dashboard or Orders page, click the "New Order" button.

2

Select Customer

Search for an existing customer by name, company, or email. The search will show matching results.

3

Add Products

Search for products to add. You can also add custom line items. Adjust quantities and prices as needed.

4

Set Delivery Details

Enter delivery address, select payment method, and set priority if needed.

5

Create Order

Review totals (VAT is calculated automatically) and click "Create Order".

Order Statuses

StatusMeaning
PENDINGNew order, awaiting confirmation
CONFIRMEDOrder confirmed, stock reserved
AWAITING_PAYMENTPOP uploaded, needs verification
PAYMENT_RECEIVEDPayment verified
PROCESSINGBeing prepared
DISPATCHEDShipped to customer
DELIVEREDOrder complete
Important: When you change status to CONFIRMED, stock is automatically deducted from inventory.

Adding a New Product

1

Click "Add Product"

From the Products page, click the "Add Product" button.

2

Enter Product Details

Fill in the product name, SKU, and select a category. The slug is auto-generated.

3

Set Pricing

Enter the selling price. Optionally add cost price (for margin tracking) and bulk pricing.

4

Set Inventory

Enter stock quantity and low stock threshold (you'll get alerts when stock falls below this).

Quick Stock Updates

From the Products list, click on the stock number to quickly update inventory without opening the full edit form.

Tip: Use the "Low Stock" filter button to quickly see all products that need restocking.

Customer Tags

Customers are automatically tagged based on their activity:

TagCriteria
NewRegistered within last 30 days
VIP5+ orders or R50,000+ spent
Repeat2 or more orders
Mining/Construction/EngineeringBased on company name

Lead Score

Each customer has a lead score (0-100) based on:

  • Number of orders (max 30 points)
  • Total spent (max 30 points)
  • Number of quotes (max 20 points)
  • Has company name (10 points)
  • Recent activity (max 10 points)

Quick Actions

From the customer list, use the quick action buttons to:

  • WhatsApp - Opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message
  • Call - Initiates phone call
  • Email - Opens email client with pre-filled subject

Creating a Quote

1

Click "New Quote"

From Dashboard or Quotes page, click "New Quote".

2

Enter Customer Details

Search for existing customer OR enter details manually for new prospects.

3

Add Items

Search and add products. Prices can be adjusted per quote.

4

Set Validity

Choose how long the quote is valid (7, 14, 30, or 60 days).

Follow-Up System

The Dashboard shows pending quotes that need follow-up:

  • Follow up - Quote is 2+ days old with no response
  • Expires in Xd - Quote expires within 3 days
Tip: Use the WhatsApp/Call/Email buttons on pending quotes to quickly follow up with customers.

Categories help organize your products. The system comes with default categories:

  • Fasteners
  • Bearings
  • Power Transmission
  • Hydraulics
  • Pneumatics
  • Tools
  • Safety Equipment
  • Electrical

Adding/Editing Categories

Click "Add Category" to create new ones. Click the edit icon on any category to modify it. The slug is auto-generated from the name.

Note: Deleting a category with products is not allowed. Reassign products first.

Navigation

  • Click the hamburger menu (three lines) to open the sidebar
  • Use the bottom navigation on mobile for quick access
  • "View Site" link opens the public website in a new tab

Keyboard Shortcuts

ActionShortcut
Search (in list pages)Type and press Enter
Cancel/Close modalClick outside or X button

Best Practices

  • Check the Dashboard daily for alerts and pending items
  • Follow up on quotes within 2-3 days
  • Keep stock levels updated to avoid overselling
  • Verify POP documents promptly
  • Use customer tags to identify VIP clients
Security: Always log out when leaving your workstation. Sessions expire after 7 days.
