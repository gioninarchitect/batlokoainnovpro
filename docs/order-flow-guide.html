Phase 1: Lead / Inquiry
Customer contact and CRM entry

Customer Makes Contact

Customer reaches out via website form, phone call, WhatsApp, or email inquiry.

Customer Created in CRM

Staff creates customer record with all relevant details:

Field Description
Name & Email Contact information
Phone For WhatsApp follow-ups
Company Business name (optional)
Tags VIP, Mining, Construction, etc.
Source Website, Referral, Walk-in
Payment Terms 0, 15, or 30 days
Pro Tip: Always add relevant tags to customers for better segmentation and follow-up targeting.
Phase 2: Quote Creation
Generate and send quotation to customer

Quote Created PENDING

Admin creates quote with line items (products or custom items), pricing, VAT calculation, and validity period.

Quote Sent SENT

Quote PDF sent to customer via email or WhatsApp. Includes payment link for when ready.

Customer Views Quote VIEWED

System tracks when customer opens the quote (if sent via tracked link).

Quote Outcomes:

ACCEPTED

Customer approves. Admin converts quote to order.

CONVERTED

REJECTED / EXPIRED

Customer declines or quote expires past valid date. Follow up or close.

Phase 3: Order Processing
Order confirmation and preparation

Order Created PENDING

Order is created (from quote conversion or direct entry):

  • Order number generated: ORD-2025-00001
  • Line items with pricing
  • Delivery address captured
  • WhatsApp alert sent to admin

Order Confirmed CONFIRMED

Staff reviews and confirms order:

  • Stock is reserved/deducted from inventory
  • Invoice can now be generated
  • Customer notified of confirmation

Awaiting Payment AWAITING_PAYMENT

Customer receives payment instructions with unique payment link.

Payment URL: /pay/ORD-2025-00001
Phase 4: Customer Payment
Customer makes payment and uploads proof

Customer Visits Payment Page

Customer accesses: /pay/ORD-2025-00001

Page displays:

  • Order total amount
  • Order status

Banking Details Displayed

Bank First National Bank (FNB)
Account Name Batlokoa Innovative Projects
Account Number 62000000000
Branch Code 250655
Reference ORD-2025-00001

Customer Uploads Proof of Payment

Customer uploads bank confirmation (JPG, PNG, or PDF up to 10MB).

  • File saved to server
  • popVerified set to false
  • WhatsApp alert sent to admin
Customer sees confirmation: "Proof of Payment Uploaded - We will verify shortly"
Phase 5: Payment Verification
Admin verifies proof of payment

Dashboard Alert

Admin dashboard shows: X POP awaiting verification

Clicking navigates to orders filtered by AWAITING_PAYMENT status.

Admin Reviews POP

Admin opens order, views uploaded file, and verifies against bank statement.

Verification Outcomes:

APPROVE POP

Payment verified successfully:

  • Status: PAYMENT_RECEIVED
  • Payment: PAID
  • popVerified: true
REJECT POP

Payment not verified (wrong amount, unclear proof):

  • Status reverts to PENDING
  • Rejection reason saved
  • Customer must re-upload
Phase 6: Order Fulfillment
Picking, packing, and delivery

Processing PROCESSING

Staff picks items from inventory and prepares packaging.

Ready for Dispatch READY_FOR_DISPATCH

Items packed and labeled, awaiting courier pickup.

Dispatched DISPATCHED

Order handed to courier:

  • Tracking number recorded
  • Courier name logged
  • dispatchedAt timestamp set

Delivered DELIVERED

Order successfully delivered to customer. Order complete!

  • deliveredAt timestamp set
  • Customer stats updated
Phase 7: Invoicing
Generate and track invoices

Invoice Generated

After payment received or on dispatch, generate invoice:

  • Invoice number: INV-2025-00001
  • Due date calculated from customer payment terms
  • PDF generated for download/email

Invoice Status

Status Meaning
PAID Payment received and verified
UNPAID Awaiting payment
OVERDUE Past due date, requires follow-up
PARTIAL Partial payment received

Complete Status Flow

PENDING → CONFIRMED → AWAITING_PAYMENT → PAYMENT_RECEIVED → PROCESSING → READY_FOR_DISPATCH → DISPATCHED → DELIVERED

Special Statuses:

CANCELLED or ON_HOLD (can occur at any point before DISPATCHED)
